NIGHTLIGHT 2


Nightlighter is a wonderful thing for the kid’s sweet dream. Falling asleep with funny animals and calm lullabies – just pleasure. Nice music influences positively on dreams. Babies won’t be afraid to stay in the dark, because the “nightlight” is an excellent alternative to the lamp. Use a timer to set the necessary turn-off time. Thanks to this App there is an opportunity to learn quickly the names of animals. Bright pictures of dog, frog, cat and others, will attract the attention of your children. There is also screen lock and practical menu in this App.
What's new?:
- Updated interface
- new tunes
- more animals
- change colors If you always try to put babies to the bed or just divert their attention for a long time – this application for you!
